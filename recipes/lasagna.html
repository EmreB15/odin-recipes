Lasagna Recipe

This is a recipe on how to create a lasagna from scratch at home

Ingredients

  • 1tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stick, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 250g beef mince
  • 250g pork mince
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 200ml beef stock
  • 200ml red wine
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 9-12 lasagna sheets
  • 50g grated parmesan
  • 150g shredded mozzarella
    • '
  • 50g butter
  • 50g plain flour
  • 550ml semi skimmed milk

Directions

  1. In large pan heat the olive oil over a low heat
  2. Fry the onion m carrot, celery and garlic for 5 mins or until softended
  3. Add the mince and fry on a medium heat until golden
  4. Turn up the heat, pour in the wine and bublle unitl reduced.
  5. Stir in the tomato puree, chopped tomatoes and stock
  6. Add in the worcestershire sauce and simmer for 15 mins or until the liquid has reduced.
  7. Season
  8. While it is simmering for 15 minutes make the white sauce
  9. Melt the butter in a small saucepan over a low heat
  10. Add the flour, whisk until comvined and cook on low for 1-2 mins
  11. Remove from the heat and gradually whisk in the milk until you have a loose sauce
  12. Season
  13. Return to a gentle heat and whisk constantly until the sauce thickens
  14. Preheat te oven to gas 6. 200C, fan 180C
  15. Layer up the lasagne in a baking dish starting with a third each of the ragu then the Pasta then the white sauce and repeat this twice
  16. Top with the cheese and then bake in the oven for 40-50 mins until piping hot
  17. Serve once finished and Enjoy
